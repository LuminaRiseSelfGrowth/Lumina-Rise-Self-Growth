My Story

The journey that led to Lumina Rise Self-Growth

Passion for Empowerment

Hi, I’m Gina-Line — the heart and soul behind Lumina Rise. After over a decade of diving deep into my own self-development journey, I felt a powerful pull to create a space where women could feel truly seen, supported, and empowered to grow into the most radiant version of themselves. I’ve walked through tough seasons, rediscovered my inner strength, and learned firsthand the life-changing power of resilience, mindset, and self-care. That’s why I’m so passionate about helping others do the same.

With a background in Positive Psychology Coaching, a Yale certificate in Well-being and an unshakable love for personal growth, I now guide women toward lives filled with purpose, confidence, and joy. Lumina Rise isn’t just a brand — it’s a movement. One rooted in authenticity, growth, and deep compassion. Whether you're here to heal, evolve, or reconnect with your true self, I’m honored to walk this path beside you.

In a world that’s always rushing, Lumina Rise offers a different pace — one that blends self-care with self-growth, and inner work with real-life action. I’m here to equip you with tools, insights, and soulful support that help you flourish, not just function.

What truly lights up my soul is witnessing that spark — the moment a woman realizes her own power and starts showing up fully for her life. That’s the kind of magic I live for.

I’m endlessly curious about how mindset, emotions, and intentional action create lasting transformation. I’m always exploring new ideas, old wisdom, and evidence-based practices that help women thrive from the inside out. That’s why I’m so drawn to positive psychology — it combines the science of well-being with the heart of meaningful living.

At my core, I’m driven by a simple but powerful passion: helping women reclaim their joy, confidence, and sense of possibility. No matter what stage of the journey you’re on, know this — you’re not alone. And your rise? It starts right here.

Where Self-Care Meets Self-Growth

This powerful statement reflects our holistic approach to women's empowerment. We recognize that self-care is not just about pampering yourself or taking a break from life's demands. It's about creating a strong foundation of physical, emotional, and mental well-being that enables you to pursue your goals and dreams with vigor and resilience.

Similarly, self-growth isn't just about pushing yourself to achieve more. It's about expanding your horizons, challenging your limitations, and evolving into the best version of yourself – all while maintaining a compassionate and nurturing attitude towards your own journey.

Our slogan embodies the idea that self-care and self-growth are not mutually exclusive, but rather complementary forces. When you learn to care for yourself deeply and grow intentionally, you create a positive feedback loop that propels you towards your highest potential. This is the essence of what I offer at Lumina Rise – a journey where every step towards growth is also an act of self-love and care.

This journey is about creating a space where you are not just surviving but thriving. When you care for yourself, you build resilience, emotional intelligence, and physical well-being—all of which are essential for sustainable growth.

This philosophy promotes the idea that growth doesn't have to be painful or overwhelming. It can be uplifting, healing, and deeply personal. When self-care is woven into your growth journey, you'll find that both become easier and more fulfilling, leading to a more meaningful life.

Education & Credentials

My commitment to continuous learning and professional development

Delinquency Intervention Techniques

Comprehensive training in the fields of psychology, sociology, and criminology, focused on understanding human behavior, social dynamics, and criminal patterns. This diploma emphasizes practical intervention strategies, conflict resolution, and relational tools for working with individuals in vulnerable or at-risk situations. With a strong foundation in both theory and applied methods, it equips professionals to support rehabilitation, foster social reintegration, and promote positive behavioral change within diverse populations.

Positive Psychology Coach Certification

Specialized training in evidence-based approaches to well-being, strengths-based development, and positive interventions that foster growth and resilience.

The Science of Well-Being – Yale University

An evidence-based course exploring the psychological science behind happiness, well-being, and human flourishing. This certification focuses on cognitive and behavioral strategies proven to increase life satisfaction, rewire mental habits, and promote sustainable personal growth. Developed by Professor Laurie Santos, the course combines insights from positive psychology and behavioral science to empower individuals with practical tools for greater emotional and mental well-being.
