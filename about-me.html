The Journey That Led to Lumina Rise Self-Growth

Through my personal and professional experiences, I've come to understand that growth is not a luxury but rather a responsibility to oneself. Lumina Rise has become the space I wish I had during certain moments of my life.

Passion for Self-Development

Hi, I’m Gina-Line, the heart and soul behind Lumina Rise. After over a decade of diving deep into my own self-development journey, I felt a powerful pull to create a space where women could feel truly seen, supported, and empowered to grow into the most radiant version of themselves.

With a background in Positive Psychology Coaching, a Yale certificate in Well-being and an unshakable love for personal growth, I now guide women toward lives filled with purpose, confidence, and joy.

In a world that’s always rushing, Lumina Rise offers a different pace, one that blends self-care with self-growth, and inner work with real-life action.
