What Is One-on-One Coaching?

Our one-on-one coaching sessions provide dedicated support tailored specifically to your unique needs, challenges, and goals. Work directly with a certified positive psychology coach who will guide you through your personal growth journey.

At Lumina Rise, we believe that transformation happens most powerfully within the context of a supportive relationship. Your coach serves as both a guide and a partner, helping you navigate obstacles, identify opportunities, and create sustainable positive change in your life.

Whether you're seeking to overcome specific challenges, develop new skills, or create a more fulfilling life aligned with your values, our personalized coaching approach meets you exactly where you are and helps you move forward with confidence.

The Coaching Experience

What to expect during your personal transformation journey

Initial Discovery Session

We begin with a comprehensive 90-minute session to understand your current situation, challenges, and what you hope to achieve. This helps us create a personalized coaching plan aligned with your needs.

Regular Sessions

Weekly or bi-weekly 60-minute sessions that include reflection on progress, exploration of challenges, practical exercises, and setting action steps for continued growth.

Ongoing Support

Email support between sessions to address questions or challenges that arise during your practice of new skills and mindsets.

Tailored Resources

Access to personalized worksheets, exercises, and resources from our various toolkits, selected specifically to support your unique journey.
