Group Workshops

Transform Together in Our Group Workshops

Our interactive workshops bring women together in a safe, supportive environment to learn, share, and grow. Each workshop focuses on specific aspects of self-care and personal growth, providing practical tools and strategies you can implement immediately in your life.

Benefits of Group Workshops

Cost-Effective Growth

Current Workshop Offerings

Building Unshakeable Confidence

A 3-hour workshop focused on identifying and overcoming limiting beliefs, developing positive self-talk, and creating daily practices that build authentic confidence from within.

Format: Interactive exercises, guided visualization, group discussion

Boundaries & Self-Care Mastery

Learn how to establish healthy boundaries, communicate your needs effectively, and create a sustainable self-care routine that supports your wellbeing without guilt or overwhelm.

Format: Role-playing exercises, practical planning, accountability pairing

Emotional Intelligence Intensive

Develop greater awareness of your emotional patterns, learn techniques to regulate difficult emotions, and enhance your ability to connect with others through empathetic communication.

Format: Self-assessment, mindfulness practices, partner exercises

Purpose & Passion Discovery

Uncover your core values, natural strengths, and deepest aspirations to align your daily life with what truly matters to you.

Format: Guided journaling, vision boarding, small group sharing

Frequently Asked Questions

What technology do I need for virtual workshops?

You'll need a computer or tablet with internet connection, webcam, and microphone. We use Zoom for our virtual workshops, which you can download for free.

How interactive are the workshops?

Very interactive! Our workshops include breakout rooms, discussions, exercises, and opportunities to share. However, sharing is always optional.

Will I receive materials before the workshop?

Yes, you'll receive a digital workbook and any preparation instructions 48 hours before the workshop.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 7 days before the workshop. Within 7 days, you can transfer your registration to a future workshop or receive workshop materials.
