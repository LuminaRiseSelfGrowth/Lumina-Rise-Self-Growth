What Are Group Workshops?

Our interactive workshops bring women together in a safe, supportive environment to learn, share, and grow. Each workshop focuses on specific aspects of self-care and personal growth, providing practical tools and strategies you can implement immediately in your life.

At Lumina Rise, we've carefully designed our workshops to balance structured learning with authentic connection. You'll gain valuable insights from both our expert facilitators and the collective wisdom of women who share similar challenges and aspirations.

Whether you join us virtually or in-person, our workshops create a transformative space where you can explore new perspectives, practice new skills, and build meaningful connections with like-minded women on similar growth journeys.
