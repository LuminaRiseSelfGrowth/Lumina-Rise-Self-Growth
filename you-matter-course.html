What is the You Matter Course?

You Matter: 21-Day Journey to Self-Worth & Inner Peace is a guided experience that gently walks you through daily reflections, mindset shifts, and tiny actions that add up to real change.

Instead of rushing, fixing, or “hustling for your worth”, you’ll learn to slow down, listen to yourself, and build a kinder inner world—one day at a time.

Every prompt and tool is rooted in positive psychology and self-compassion, designed to help you feel seen, supported, and empowered.

What You’ll Walk Away With

Small, meaningful shifts that change the way you speak to yourself, hold space for your needs, and move through your days.

Stronger Self-Worth

Reconnect with your value beyond what you do for others, and start believing that you are already enough.

More Inner Peace

Learn simple calming tools you can use on stressful days to ground yourself and choose peace over pressure.

Softer Self-Talk

Gently shift the way you talk to yourself, so your inner voice becomes more supportive and less critical.

Tiny Daily Habits

Build micro-habits you can actually keep, instead of all-or-nothing routines that burn out after a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still wondering if this is the right fit for you? Here are a few answers that might help.

How much time does it take each day?

Most days take about 10–20 minutes. The goal is to keep it realistic so you can show up consistently, even on busy weeks.

Do I have to start on a specific date?

No. Once you have access, you can begin whenever you’re ready and move at your own pace. You can also repeat the journey as often as you like.

What if I miss a day?

That’s completely okay. This course is built from a place of self-compassion, not perfection. You can pause, come back, and continue where you left off.
